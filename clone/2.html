About Our University

Thal University Bhakkar is a public sector university that provides advanced, intellectual learning that illuminates the human minds and broadens the vision of bringing back home all kinds of opportunities and development. It was established in February 2022 under the Act of the Provincial Assembly and has more than 3000 students currently enrolled in 18 departments. Thal University has maintained a high standard in providing services. It has well-equipped classrooms, state-of-the-art IT labs, and libraries. Other facilities like hostels for students, sports grounds, transport facilities, Jamia Mosque, photocopying and thesis/paper binding, canteen, etc. are located at the University.
